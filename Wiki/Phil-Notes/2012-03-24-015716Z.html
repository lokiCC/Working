Division of Reality

Date: 3/24/12
Time: 1:57 AM
Topic: Division of Reality

Our tendency to divide the perceived world into individual and separate things and to experience ourselves as isolated egos in this world is seen as an illusion which comes from our measuring and categorizing mentality. It is called Avidya, or ignorance in Buddhist philosophy, and is seen as the state of a disturbed mind which has to be overcome.
