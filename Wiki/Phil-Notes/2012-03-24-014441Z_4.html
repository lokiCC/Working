Sils-Maria

Date: 3/24/12
Time: 1:44 AM
Topic: Sils-Maria

I sat there waiting, waiting – not for anything. Beyond good and evil, enjoying soon the light.

Soon the shade, now only play, now The lake, now the noon, wholly time without end.

Then suddenly, friend, one became two – And Zarathustra passed by me.

Nietzsche
