Eastern Image of the Divine

Date: 3/24/12
Time: 1:57 AM
Topic: Eastern Image of the Divine

The eastern image of the divine is not that of a ruler who directs the world from above, but of a principle that controls everything from within:

He who, dwelling in all things, Yet is other than all things, Whom all things do not know, Who controls all things from within- He is your Soul, the Inner Controller, The Immortal

P.11
